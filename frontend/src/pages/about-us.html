Our Story
Detail Geeks started with a simple idea: car detailing should be easy, convenient, and feel like it was done by someone who cares. At our mobile car detailing company, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service right to your doorstep. Our team is dedicated to making your vehicle look its best, whether you're a busy parent, a car lover, or just want your ride looking fresh.
Meet the Owner
Hey, I’m Cameron  Braun
I am 16 years old and the founder of Detail Geeks Auto Spa
I started this business because I believe car detailing should be convenient, personal, and done with real care. Too many places rush through the job — I take the time to do it right.
As someone who recently got their first car, I know that feeling that you only get when you get a new car. With Detail Geeks, I want to make that feeling possible for everyone without having to buy a new car. That is why I bring high-quality, reliable service straight to your driveway, treating every car like it’s my own. I value trust, consistency, and making sure every customer drives away happy.
Thank you for supporting my business. I’m excited to work with you soon!
